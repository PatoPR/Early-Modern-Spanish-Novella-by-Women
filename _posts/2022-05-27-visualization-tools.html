At our website, we're committed to using the latest and most innovative tools to enhance your experience and deepen your understanding of the works of Spanish women writers. To that end, we've embedded various powerful tools directly into our site, allowing you to explore and engage with these texts in new and exciting ways.

One of our favourite tools is Voyant Tools, which provides a suite of text analysis and visualization tools that allow you to delve deep into these texts' language, themes, and structures. With Voyant Tools, you can explore word frequency, word clouds, and other visualizations that help bring these works to life.

We've also integrated Storyline and Timeline from Knight Lab, two powerful tools that allow you to explore these writers' historical context and evolution and their works. With Storyline, you can explore the key events and trends that shaped their lives and influenced their writing, while Timeline provides a detailed chronology of their works and significant milestones.

We believe these tools add a new dimension to the study of Spanish women's literature, and we're excited to share them with you. We invite you to explore our site and discover the rich literary heritage of these remarkable writers, using these cutting-edge tools to deepen your understanding and appreciation of their works.
