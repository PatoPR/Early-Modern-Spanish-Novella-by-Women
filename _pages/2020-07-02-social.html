Want to schedule your own Happy Hour or Coffee Break?

One of the great things about conferences is the people we get to meet. An online conference requires a little more effort, but you can schedule a meetup, either on a specific topic or in general, as a group, or 1-on-1 on Zoom. You can also try out Jitsi Meet, an open-source, fully encrypted video conferencing service. More information about how to use Jitsi here.
